A — Portal Landing Page

Home screen shows all 6 markets as large cards. Click to enter a full-screen dedicated workspace per market. Strong identity for each market. Best for deliberate, focused analysis sessions.

B — Icon Rail Sidebar

Persistent left rail of market icons (like VS Code). One click switches markets instantly — no landing page. Workspace fills the rest of the screen. Best for analysts who jump between markets frequently.

C — Top Tab Bar

Markets as top-level tabs, with per-market sub-tabs below. Familiar browser-like pattern. Compact but can get crowded. Nested tabs (markets → views) adds cognitive overhead.

My recommendation

A + B hybrid: Portal landing for first load (dramatic, bookmarkable), then once inside a market the icon rail appears for instant switching. Best of both — great first impression, fast analyst workflow.
