Sub-project 8 — Which market next?

The Global Market Hub has 7 markets. Which direction should we go?

A

₿ Crypto Market

Bitcoin, Ethereum, and top altcoins — price dashboard, dominance chart, Fear & Greed index, and on-chain supply metrics. Free data via CoinGecko public API. A major modern asset class that belongs alongside commodities and FX.

Sub-views: Price Dashboard · Dominance Chart · Market Cap Heatmap · On-Chain Metrics
B

🌐 Global Macro Dashboard

Cross-country GDP growth, inflation, central bank policy rates, current account balances, and government debt/GDP. The "10,000 ft view" that puts all other markets in context. Powered by FRED + World Bank data.

Sub-views: Growth & Inflation · Policy Rates · Trade Balances · Debt Monitor
C

👷 Labor & Employment Market

Jobs reports, unemployment rates, wage growth, productivity, and participation rates across the US and major economies. The human side of the macro picture — directly relevant to Fed policy and consumer spending.

Sub-views: Jobs Dashboard · Wage Tracker · Participation Rates · Fed Watch

My recommendation: B (Global Macro) — it's the analytical backbone that gives context to every other market. Bonds, FX, commodities, and equities all react to macro signals. A dedicated macro view turns the hub into a true cross-market analysis platform.
