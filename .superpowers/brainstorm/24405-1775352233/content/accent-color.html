Commodities — Accent Color

Each market has its own accent color for tabs, status indicators, spinners, and pill values. The 5 existing markets are shown for reference.

FX — Amber Bonds — Green Real Estate — Orange Derivatives — Purple Insurance — Sky Blue
A

Gold — #ca8a04

Classic commodity color. Evokes precious metals and the gold standard. Warm and recognizable. Slightly darker than FX amber so they stay distinct.

B

Copper — #b87333

The "economic metal" — copper prices track global growth. Earthy, industrial feel. Unique in the palette, sits between orange and brown.

C

Lime — #84cc16

Agriculture and energy (biofuels). Bright and distinctive — nothing else in the palette is lime-green. High contrast on dark backgrounds.

D

Teal — #0d9488

Neutral and professional. Sits between green (Bonds) and sky blue (Insurance) but is distinct from both. Works well for a broad multi-sector view.

My recommendation: A (Gold) — intuitively tied to commodities as an asset class, and clearly distinct from all 5 existing accent colors.
